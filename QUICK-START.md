# Farm Manager - Quick Start Guide

## What is Farm Manager?

Farm Manager is a simple tool that helps you:
- Track how much you spend on farming
- Record your harvest and sales
- See which crops make the most money
- Plan better for next season

## How to Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Open the Application
- Open `farm-manager.html` in any browser
- On phone: Use Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- On computer: Any modern browser works

### Step 2: Add Your First Field
1. Click **"Fields & Crops"** on the left menu
2. Click **"+ Add New Field"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Field Name**: North Field (or any name)
   - **Size**: 2.5 (in hectares)
   - **Crop**: Choose from list (Maize, Tobacco, etc.)
4. Click **"Save Field"**

### Step 3: Record Your Costs
1. Click **"Inputs & Costs"** on the left
2. Click **"+ Record Input/Cost"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Date**: When you bought/paid
   - **Field**: Select your field
   - **Category**: Seeds, Fertilizer, Labour, etc.
   - **Description**: What you bought
   - **Cost**: Amount in MK (Malawi Kwacha)
4. Click **"Save Input"**

### Step 4: Record Your Harvest
1. Click **"Harvest & Yield"** on the left
2. Click **"+ Record Harvest"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Date**: When you harvested
   - **Field**: Select your field
   - **Quantity**: Weight in kilograms
   - **Quality**: Premium, Good, Fair, or Poor
4. Click **"Save Harvest"**

### Step 5: Record Your Sales
1. Click **"Sales & Revenue"** on the left
2. Click **"+ Record Sale"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Date**: When you sold
   - **Field**: Select your field
   - **Quantity**: Kilograms sold
   - **Price per kg**: Price in MK
   - **Buyer**: Who bought (ADMARC, trader, etc.)
4. Click **"Save Sale"**

### Step 6: See Your Results
1. Click **"Dashboard"** to see totals
2. Click **"Analytics"** to see which crops made money

## Important Tips

### ‚úÖ Do This
- **Record everything**: Even small costs add up
- **Write things down immediately**: Don't wait and forget
- **Be accurate**: Measure field sizes and harvest weights properly
- **Keep receipts**: Match your records with receipts
- **Export data often**: Save backup files

### ‚ùå Don't Do This
- **Don't clear browser data**: You'll lose your records
- **Don't share device**: Keep your farm data private
- **Don't skip small costs**: Everything counts
- **Don't guess**: Estimate carefully if you must

## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need internet?**  
A: No! It works completely offline once you open it.

**Q: Can I use it on my phone?**  
A: Yes! It works great on smartphones.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: On your device only. Nothing goes to the internet.

**Q: How do I backup my data?**  
A: Go to "Export Data" and click "Download All Data"

**Q: Can I use it for multiple seasons?**  
A: Yes! Keep using it season after season.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**  
A: Export your data, edit the file, and reload. (Edit feature coming soon)

**Q: Can my wife/brother also use it?**  
A: Each device has separate data. For sharing, use one device together.

**Q: Does it work with livestock?**  
A: Not yet. Livestock features coming in next version.

## Example: Full Season Workflow

### November (Planting Time)
1. Add fields: "North 2ha Maize", "South 1.5ha Groundnuts"
2. Record costs:
   - Hybrid maize seeds: MK 45,000
   - NPK fertilizer (basal): MK 120,000
   - Planting labour: MK 15,000

### December (Early Growing)
1. Record costs:
   - Weeding labour: MK 12,000
   - Pesticide: MK 8,000

### January (Mid Growing)
1. Record costs:
   - Top dressing fertilizer: MK 80,000
   - Weeding labour: MK 12,000

### April (Harvest Time)
1. Record harvest:
   - North Field: 4,500 kg maize
   - South Field: 2,100 kg groundnuts
2. Record costs:
   - Harvest labour: MK 25,000
   - Transport: MK 15,000

### May-June (Selling)
1. Record sales:
   - 3,000 kg maize @ MK 280/kg = MK 840,000
   - 1,500 kg groundnuts @ MK 850/kg = MK 1,275,000
2. Check Analytics:
   - See which crop was more profitable
   - Calculate profit per hectare
   - Plan for next season

## Getting Help

### If Something Doesn't Work
1. Refresh the browser page
2. Make sure you saved the data (click Save buttons)
3. Check you're on the right page/field
4. Export your data to be safe

### For Technical Issues
- Make sure browser is updated
- Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Check device has enough storage space
- Don't use private/incognito mode
